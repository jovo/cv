I am currently an Assistant Professor of Biomedical Engineering in the Whiting School of Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, where I co-direct the \href{https://neurodata.io/}{NeuroData} lab, whose mission is to flourish together by extending and fusing statistical machine learning and big data science to address the most important brain science and mental health questions of our time. As of February 2019, according to \href{https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DWPfdT4AAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao}{Google Scholar}, I have over 4,100 citations and an h-index of 28.
